## Apperance

* Click Customize
* Go to All settings
  * Open Editor on the side
  * Unfold General
  * Go to Code Folding
    * uncheck Imports under the general section
    * uncheck closures
    * uncheck Generic constructor
  * Go to Appearance
    * Make sure line numbers is checked
